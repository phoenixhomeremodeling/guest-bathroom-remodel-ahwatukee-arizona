What is the best color for a guest bathroom?

To establish a tranquil, spa-like atmosphere in your bathroom, consider using calming neutral shades such as blue, green, and gray, drawing inspiration from nature with hues like soft sage, sky blue, and ash gray, or, for a cozier ambiance, opt for warmer tones like pale yellow, warm beige, or a subtle blush color to impart a welcoming radiance to the space.

What adds the most value to a bathroom?

To maximize the value of your bathroom renovation, consider these steps: replace the tub with a spa shower, install a vanity with ample storage, replace the toilet, opt for classic tile, refresh the grout, prioritize appropriate lighting, select soothing color schemes, and coordinate metal finishes.

How much does it cost to redo a small bathroom?

The cost of a typical small bathroom remodel typically falls around $6,300, with average prices ranging from $2,500 to $10,000, and factors such as the size of the space, choice of materials, labor expenses, and other considerations can influence the overall project cost.

What comes first in bathroom remodeling?

As the first step in a bathroom renovation, if you're either partially or completely remodeling the space, begin by removing bathroom fixtures and drywall; while gutting the bathroom is not overly complex, it's essential to follow specific steps to prevent costly errors during the demolition stage.
